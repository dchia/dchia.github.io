Projects

Simple Lists


This is an Android app that allows users to create an unlimited number of lists with items that are simple to add, edit, rearrange, and delete. I implemented drag and drop, swipe, and double-tap gesture capabilities. Furthermore, no permissions are needed, and the app requires only very little memory.

MyFinances


This Android app is a personal finances tracker that helps users to keep track of their income, expenses, loans, and accounts receivables. I implemented automated recording and reminders features that are synced with Google Calendar for recurring transactions such as salary and rent. Trend graphs to allow users to set goals and predict time until goal achievement is a work in progress.
